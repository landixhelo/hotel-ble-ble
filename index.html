HOTEL BLE BLE

Read Few About Us

Offering an on-site restaurant, Hotel Ble Ble is located in Vlorë, just 100 m from the nearest beach. The hotel features a garden and barbecue facilities, while free WiFi is provided throughout.

Each room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a balcony. Featuring a bath, private bathrooms also come with a bidet. You can enjoy sea view and mountain view from the room.
